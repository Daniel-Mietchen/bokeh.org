We are grateful to our sponsors, whose generous support helps ensure the sustainability of the Bokeh project.


If your company uses Bokeh and is able to sponsor the project, please contact info@bokeh.org. If you are a user who appreciates Bokeh and want to support our mission, please consider making a donation to support our efforts.

Bokeh was initially developed with funding from the DARPA XDATA Program, and subsequently with support from Anaconda, Inc. Bokeh is now a fiscally sponsored project of NumFOCUS, a nonprofit dedicated to supporting the open source scientific computing community.
